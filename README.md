Orders collapse.

This was the business crisis scenario behind my latest Power BI case study:

# 🍔 QuickBite Business Impact & Recovery Analysis

Built as part of the [Codebasics Resume Project Challenge] 

The challenge simulated a real-world crisis faced by a Bengaluru-based food-tech startup after:
⚠️ food safety incidents
⚠️ delivery outages during monsoon season
⚠️ aggressive competitor campaigns

Using Power BI, I designed a complete multi-page business intelligence dashboard to analyze the crisis impact and recommend recovery strategies.

# 📊 Dashboard Highlights

## 📌 Business Overview

* Revenue dropped by nearly **71%**
* Order volume declined significantly during the crisis phase
* Cancellation rate doubled during operational disruptions

## 📈 Order Trends Analysis

* Orders dropped from ~23K/month to below 9K/month
* ~72% of orders came from weekdays, showing heavy weekday dependency
* Clear behavioral shift after the crisis period

## 🚚 Delivery Performance Insights

* Average delivery time increased from ~40 mins to ~60 mins
* Delivery delays reached ~64% late deliveries
* Operational inefficiencies strongly impacted customer retention

## 😊 Customer Sentiment Analysis

* Ratings and sentiment scores dropped sharply after June 2025
* Negative reviews increased consistently during the crisis phase
* Customer trust deteriorated alongside delivery failures

## 🍽️ Restaurant Performance Insights

* Restaurants with moderate prep time categories handled the highest order volume
* Some restaurant partnerships remained more resilient during the crisis
* Partner performance directly affected customer satisfaction trends

## 💰 Revenue Impact Analysis

* Revenue fell sharply despite increased discounting efforts
* Promotions alone failed to recover customer trust
* Operational recovery proved more important than aggressive offers


# 🚀 Strategic Recovery Recommendations

Based on the analysis, I proposed a recovery roadmap focused on:

✅ Rebuilding delivery reliability
✅ Strengthening restaurant quality & safety monitoring
✅ Launching targeted customer win-back campaigns
✅ Improving customer communication transparency
✅ Prioritizing high-performing restaurant partnerships
